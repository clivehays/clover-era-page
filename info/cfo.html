The Cost Gap

Most companies track turnover cost at the recruiting invoice: 0.25-0.5x annual salary per departure.

The actual cost is 4-8x higher when you include what gets missed:

Productivity ramp (3-6 months to full output) 0.5-0.75x salary
Project delays and missed deadlines 0.25-0.5x salary
Knowledge and relationship loss 0.3-0.6x salary
Team strain and subsequent departures 0.5-1.0x salary
True cost per departure 1.5-3x annual salary
