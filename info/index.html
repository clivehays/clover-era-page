Turnover prevention for tech companies

The Problem

You find out someone is leaving when they hand in notice. By then, they've already accepted another offer. Counteroffers rarely work because the decision was made weeks ago.

Most companies track turnover at $25K-$50K per departure. The actual cost is $180K-$400K when you include productivity ramp, project delays, knowledge loss, and team strain.

What Clover ERA Does

We detect resignation intent 60-90 days before notice. Early enough to intervene. Late enough to be accurate.

How It Works

  • Daily anonymous signals from employees (30 seconds)
  • Pattern detection identifies disengagement before it becomes a decision
  • Manager alerts with specific actions to take
  • Leadership dashboard showing organization-wide risk

The Result

Prevent departures instead of reacting to them. Retain your best people before they start looking.
