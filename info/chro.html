Clover ERA

Turn retention from reactive to proactive

The Challenge You Know Too Well

You see the warning signs before anyone else. But by the time leadership acts, it's too late. Exit interviews confirm what you already knew. Annual surveys miss the moment. Managers come to you after the resignation, not before.

What Changes With Clover ERA

  • Continuous signal, not annual snapshot — daily anonymous input (30 seconds)
  • Managers get alerts directly — with specific actions, not just data
  • 60-90 day warning window — intervention happens before the decision is made
  • Organization-wide visibility — see risk concentration before it becomes attrition

The Employee Experience

  • 30 seconds daily — one rotating question, not a 45-minute survey
  • Truly anonymous — managers see patterns, not individual responses
  • They see action — because managers intervene before it's too late

How It Fits

Clover ERA doesn't replace your engagement surveys or HR systems. It fills the gap between annual measurement and daily reality. It gives managers the early warning they need to act, and gives you visibility into where risk is building across the organization.

The Outcome

Retention becomes a proactive capability, not a reactive function. You stop explaining why people left and start preventing them from leaving.
