Common Setup Questions

How is complete anonymity maintained? +

We only store the team lead/manager's name and email address. Employees join using your team code without creating accounts or sharing any personal information. All feedback is 100% anonymous - we never collect names, emails, or any identifying data from team members.

Do employees need to create accounts? +

No! Employees simply download the Clover Era app, enter your team code, and start providing feedback immediately. No registration, no personal details, no passwords. This ensures complete anonymity and makes it incredibly easy for them to participate.

What if we have multiple teams or departments? +

You can create multiple team codes for different departments or groups. Each code maintains separate anonymous feedback streams while still rolling up to your main dashboard for organization-wide insights.

How quickly do we see results? +

Initial scoring begins populating your dashboard within 24 hours of team members starting to use the app. Your first comprehensive report with detailed insights and recommended actions will be available after 2 weeks of data collection.

Is the app available for both iOS and Android? +

Yes! The Clover Era app is available on both the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android). Employees can use whatever device they prefer, ensuring maximum participation.

What kind of questions do employees answer? +

Quick 30-second wellbeing check-ins about stress levels, workload, team dynamics, and overall satisfaction. Questions are scientifically designed to identify burnout, toxicity, and flight risks while maintaining complete anonymity.
