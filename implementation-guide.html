Common Setup Questions

Do we really need zero IT involvement? +

Absolutely zero. Clover Era is 100% cloud-based and requires no integration with your existing systems. HR or executive teams can complete setup without any technical assistance. If you can use email, you can set up Clover Era.

What if we have 10,000+ employees? +

Large organizations can still set up in 15 minutes. Simply upload your employee CSV (we handle files with 100,000+ rows instantly), and our system auto-organizes everything. The process is identical whether you have 50 or 50,000 employees.

How does it work without accessing our systems? +

Our AI analyzes patterns through employee engagement with the platform, survey responses, and behavioral indicators—all collected with explicit consent. No access to your email, Slack, or internal systems required. Complete insights without invasion.

Is the data secure without our IT security? +

Yes. We use bank-level AES-256 encryption, are SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and undergo regular third-party security audits. Your data is likely more secure with us than on your own servers.

Can employees use it without training? +

Yes. Employees simply click the invitation link and answer occasional wellbeing check-ins (takes 30 seconds). No app download, no login hassles, no training needed. It's designed to be invisible yet impactful.

What if we need to add/remove employees? +

Changes take seconds. Add new employees individually or bulk upload. Remove departing employees with one click. Set up auto-sync with your HRIS later if desired, but it's never required.
