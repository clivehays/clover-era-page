Why Nothing Has Worked

Executive coaching

Works on individuals in isolation. The VP gets better at communication. Nobody else changes. The system stays broken.

Team building events

Create temporary connection. People bond over escape rooms and trust falls. Then they return to the same environment. The patterns reassert themselves within days.

Personality assessments

Give people labels. "I'm a D, you're an I, that's why we clash." Labels explain behavior. They don't change it.

Training programs

Teach skills in a classroom. Active listening. Giving feedback. People learn the frameworks. They don't transfer to real situations under real pressure.

Consulting firms

Diagnose beautifully. They produce 80-page decks showing exactly what's wrong. Then they leave.

The pattern is always the same: Insight without integration. Awareness without accountability. Events without environments.

Understanding the problem is not the same as solving it.
