Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Early Adopter Program

What exactly is the Early Adopter Program? +
The Early Adopter Program is an exclusive opportunity for the first 100 companies to join Clover ERA. Members receive lifetime discounts (40-50% off), priority access to new features, direct input on our product roadmap, and comprehensive success support. It's designed for forward-thinking organizations ready to lead the workplace wellbeing revolution.
How long do the benefits last? +
Forever! Your early adopter discount and benefits are locked in for the lifetime of your account. This includes your discounted rate, priority support, and early access to features. Even as we add new capabilities and our prices increase for new customers, you'll always maintain your early adopter advantages.
What if we need to scale beyond our initial tier? +
Your early adopter discount applies to all upgrades! If you start as a Starter Pioneer and grow to need Enterprise features, your 40% discount transfers and you can upgrade to maintain proportional savings. We want to support your growth, not penalize it.
Is there a commitment period? +
We offer flexible terms with no long-term contracts required. Monthly plans are available with the option to save even more with annual billing. You can cancel anytime, though we're confident you'll want to stay once you see the results. Most early adopters see positive ROI within 60-90 days.
What kind of support do early adopters receive? +
Early adopters receive our highest level of support including dedicated success managers, priority 24/7 support, quarterly business reviews, custom training sessions, and direct access to our founding team. You're not just a customer - you're a partner in building the future of workplace wellbeing.
