Features are identical. Company tiers give you volume pricing (30-40% savings), a dedicated success manager, HRIS integrations, and a single invoice for procurement.
Do you offer monthly billing for company tiers?
Company tiers are billed annually. This ensures enough time to demonstrate ROI. Team tier offers monthly billing with no commitment.
What if we have more than 500 employees?
Enterprise+ pricing starts at $96,000/year for 500+ employees. Schedule a call and we'll build a custom quote based on your needs.

Team tier is instant. Sign up and your team can start within minutes. Company tiers typically launch within 2 weeks, including onboarding and training.
What's included in the Turnover Analysis call?
A 15-minute conversation where we look at your team structure, estimate your real turnover costs (most companies track only 30% of the actual number), and identify which teams may be at highest risk. No pitch. Just math.
What's the difference between Team + Coaching and Full Team Coaching?
Team + Coaching is ongoing monthly support. You get a coach who helps you use the platform and navigate difficult situations. Full Team Coaching is a 10-week intensive that transforms how your entire team works together, including individual coaching for every member.
