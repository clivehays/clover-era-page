Common questions

What counts as a team?▼
A team is any group of up to 20 people reporting to or working with one manager. If you have a manager with 15 direct reports, that's one team.
What happens after the 14-day free trial?▼
You choose to continue or you don't. No contracts. No cancellation fees. Monthly billing unless you choose annual.
Can I start with one team and add more later?▼
Yes. Most organizations start with one or two teams, see the patterns in the bi-weekly reports, and expand from there. Your per-team rate adjusts automatically as you add teams.
How is this different from an annual engagement survey?▼
Annual surveys tell you what people thought months ago. Clover ERA shows you what's happening this week. Daily check-ins take employees under 30 seconds. Managers get bi-weekly reports showing real-time team patterns. The average employee has already decided to leave 67 days before they resign. Annual surveys miss that entire window.
What does the Free Turnover Analysis include?▼
15 minutes with our founder Clive Hays. He'll run your company's numbers through our turnover cost model and show you the gap between what you're tracking and the real financial impact. No pitch. If the numbers don't surprise you, you'll know in the first five minutes.
Do employees know their responses are tracked?▼
Responses are anonymous. Managers see team-level patterns, never individual responses. This is what creates the psychological safety for honest input.
