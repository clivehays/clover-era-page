Pricing Questions

What if the pilot doesn't work?
If we don't prevent at least 2 departures in your pilot department within 90 days, you simply don't expand. Most pilots prevent 2-3 departures (saving $240K-$540K), which makes the expansion decision obvious. We've never had a pilot fail to show positive ROI.
Can we skip the pilot and go straight to enterprise?
Yes. Some companies with clear turnover crises across multiple departments choose to implement company-wide immediately. However, 70% of our clients start with a pilot to prove ROI in their highest-risk area first, then expand with internal buy-in and documented success.
What's included in the $24K pilot?
Full implementation (not a trial): complete system setup, manager training, daily anonymous check-ins for 20-50 people, weekly reports, monthly executive briefings, and a dedicated success manager. At Day 75, we provide an ROI report and expansion roadmap based on your results.
How do you calculate ROI?
We track prevented departures. Each prevented departure saves $120K-$180K (1.5-2x salary in recruiting, training, and lost productivity). For example: $24K pilot prevents 3 departures = $360K-$540K saved = 15-22x ROI. We document this with specific examples from your team.
Do you offer monthly pricing?
No. Preventing turnover requires at least 90 days to implement, identify patterns, and enable interventions. Monthly pricing creates the wrong incentives and doesn't allow enough time to demonstrate ROI. Our shortest engagement is a 90-day pilot at $24K.
What if we need more than 500 people?
Enterprise pricing scales based on company size. For 500-1000 employees, pricing ranges from $180K-$300K annually. We'll provide a custom quote based on your specific needs during the analysis call. The ROI math still works: preventing 40-60 departures = $4.8M-$10.8M saved.
