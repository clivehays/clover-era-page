Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

What is a "unit" in your pricing? ▼
A unit is a team or department of up to 20 people. For example, if you have 45 employees, you would need 3 units. This structure allows you to organize your teams naturally while keeping costs predictable.
Can I change plans anytime? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties, no hidden fees.
What's included in the 30-day free trial? ▼
Your free trial includes full access to all features in your selected plan. No credit card required to start. You'll see real results before you pay a penny.
How do volume discounts work? ▼
As you grow, you save more: 11-25 units get 5% off, 26-50 units get 10% off, 51-100 units get 15% off, and 101+ units get 20% off. Discounts apply automatically.
What kind of ROI can I expect? ▼
Our clients typically see 3-5x ROI within 90 days through reduced turnover (47% average reduction), increased productivity (23% average increase), and lower burnout-related costs. Use our ROI calculator for personalized projections.
