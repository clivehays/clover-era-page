Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI detect mental health issues? +

Our AI analyzes behavioral patterns, communication changes, performance fluctuations, absence patterns, and interaction dynamics to identify mental health struggles 3 months before crisis. All analysis respects privacy and focuses on workplace indicators only.

Is employee privacy protected? +

Absolutely. We analyze patterns, not personal content. All data is anonymized and aggregated. Managers receive risk alerts without specific diagnoses. Employees control their own health information, and all support is voluntary and confidential.

What if employees don't want help? +

Support is always voluntary. However, 87% accept help when offered compassionately and confidentially. We focus on creating environments where seeking help is normalized and celebrated, not stigmatized.

Can this really prevent crisis situations? +

Yes. Early intervention reduces crisis incidents by 89%. By identifying struggling employees 3 months early and providing support, we prevent escalation to crisis. Many organizations achieve zero crisis incidents after implementation.

How do managers handle mental health conversations? +

We provide comprehensive manager training on mental health awareness, compassionate communication, and appropriate boundaries. Managers learn to recognize signs, offer support, and connect employees with professional resources.
