Section 04Common questions

What buyers ask before they start.

  • What counts as a team?

    A team is any group of up to 20 people reporting to or working with one manager. If you have a manager with 15 direct reports, that is one team.

  • What happens after the 14-day free trial?

    You choose to continue or you do not. No contracts. No cancellation fees. Monthly billing unless you choose annual.

  • Can I start with one team and add more later?

    Yes. Most organisations start with one or two teams, see the patterns in the bi-weekly reports, and expand from there. Your per-team rate adjusts automatically as you add teams.

  • How is this different from an annual engagement survey?

    Annual surveys tell you what people thought months ago. Clover ERA shows you what is happening this week. Daily check-ins take employees under 30 seconds. Managers get bi-weekly reports showing real-time team patterns. The average employee has decided to leave 67 days before they resign. Annual surveys miss that entire window.

  • What does the Cohort Conversation include?

    Fifteen minutes with one of our founders. They run your company's numbers through the cohort cost model and show you the gap between what you are tracking and the real financial impact. No pitch. If the numbers do not surprise you, you will know in the first five minutes.

  • Are responses really anonymous?

    Yes. The platform is built such that individual responses cannot be re-identified, including by the company commissioning the assessment. Team-level aggregates are reported only when team size is sufficient to preserve anonymity (minimum 5 respondents per team).

  • Why per-team pricing instead of per-seat?

    Pricing per team rather than per seat means the cost stays predictable as your headcount grows. A 200-person company with 12 teams pays the same whether the average team is 12 or 17 people. Per-seat pricing penalises company growth; per-team pricing rewards it.
