Six Dimensions

Six measurable signals. Each scored out of 20.

Most assessments give you one number that tells you nothing. The MGI gives you six. Each dimension surfaces a specific kind of blindness or capability. You see exactly where the gap is, not just that it exists.

  1. Visibility Can you see what's actually happening on your teams?
  2. Action Do you respond to what you see, or watch it happen?
  3. Accountability Are managers held to a standard, or rated on activity?

About the Assessment

  • How long does it take?

    10 minutes if you focus. The questions are calibrated to surface real signals, not to test you. There's no right answer, and you can't game it without knowing what we're measuring.

  • Who sees the data?

    You. The assessment is designed for the manager taking it. If you're commissioning the assessment for a team or organisation, the team-level data is anonymous and aggregated; individuals are never identified. The full data policy is at /privacy.

  • What does it cost?

    The assessment is free. Pricing for the platform that runs continuously after the assessment starts at $24K for a 90-day pilot. Most companies who complete the assessment book a 15-minute conversation to discuss what their score means in context.

  • What happens after?

    You get your score and report immediately. You can stop there if you want, or talk to us about what the platform does next. We don't follow up aggressively; the report does most of the persuading on its own.
