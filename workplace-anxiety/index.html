Box Breathing Technique
Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat 4 times to instantly reduce workplace anxiety.
5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
Name 5 things you see, 4 you touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Grounds you during anxiety spikes.
Two-Minute Mindfulness
Set a timer for 2 minutes, focus only on your breath. Proven to reduce cortisol and improve mental clarity.

Time-Blocking for Deep Work

Email Batching
Check emails only 3 times daily at set times. Reduces anxiety from constant notifications and improves focus.
Walking Meetings
Take calls while walking. Movement reduces anxiety hormones and improves creative thinking by 60%.
