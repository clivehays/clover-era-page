Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from an engagement survey?

Surveys happen once or twice a year. By the time you get results, people have already left. Clover ERA gives you weekly data and specific actions. It's a continuous read, not a snapshot.

Will my team know their individual scores?

Team members see their own responses and personal insights. You see patterns at the team level and individual signals that suggest someone may be drifting. It's designed to start conversations, not to surveil.
