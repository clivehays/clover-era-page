Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from our annual engagement survey?

Annual surveys tell you how people FELT three months ago. Clover ERA tells you how they feel TODAY, when you can still do something about it. Plus, nobody fills out surveys honestly anyway. Our anonymous daily check-ins get real signal because they're quick, safe, and actually lead to action.

Will managers actually use this?

It takes 30 seconds per day. We've had 94% daily usage rates because managers see immediate value. When the system tells them "Sarah is showing signs of disengagement," they can intervene before she starts interviewing elsewhere. Managers love tools that make their job easier. This does.

What if it doesn't work for us?

On our Growth and Enterprise tiers, we guarantee 15-25% turnover reduction in 90 days. If you don't hit it, we extend your engagement at no cost until you do. We can make this guarantee because the CLOVER Framework works when implemented properly.

Can we start with just one department first?

We've found that pilot programs fail because they don't get executive commitment. Retention is a company-wide issue that requires company-wide solutions. That said, if you want to phase the rollout, we can implement in stages over 60 days while maintaining full company coverage.

How long until we see results?

Most clients identify their first at-risk employee within 7 days. First prevented departure typically happens within 30 days. Measurable turnover reduction shows up in your 90-day numbers. This isn't a "wait 18 months to see if the culture shifts" program. You see impact immediately.

What makes you different from other employee engagement platforms?

Three things: (1) We focus on daily manager behaviors, not annual programs. (2) We're built by someone with 20+ years of Fortune 500 transformation experience who knows what actually works. (3) We guarantee outcomes instead of just selling software and hoping for the best.
