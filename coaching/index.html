Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from the $495/month Team tier?
Team tier gives you the platform. You see your Belonging Score and get recommendations, but you're on your own to act on them. Team + Coaching adds a dedicated coach who helps you interpret the data, prepare for difficult conversations, and stay accountable to your action plan.
How is this different from Full Team Coaching ($50-150K)?
Full Team Coaching is an intensive 10-week program that includes individual coaching for every team member plus team workshops. It's a full transformation. Team + Coaching is ongoing support for you as the manager. Your team uses the platform; you get the coaching.
Why a 6-month commitment?
Changing team dynamics takes time. One or two coaching sessions won't move the needle. Six months gives us enough runway to identify patterns, implement changes, and see results. After 6 months, you can continue month-to-month or transition to Team tier.
What if I need to cancel early?
The 6-month commitment is firm. We structure our coaching capacity around it. If something truly unexpected happens (company closes, you leave your role), talk to us and we'll figure it out. But "I changed my mind" isn't a reason to cancel.
How are coaches matched?
We consider your industry, team size, specific challenges, and timezone. Right now with three coaches, the match is straightforward. As we add coaches, we'll be more deliberate about fit.
Can I upgrade from Team tier to Team + Coaching?
Yes. If you're already on Team tier and want to add coaching, we'll credit your current month toward the coaching subscription. Apply and mention you're an existing customer.
What happens in the coaching sessions?
It depends on what you need. Typical sessions include: reviewing your team's Belonging Score data, preparing for a difficult conversation, debriefing after one, working through a specific team dynamic, or planning your approach for the coming weeks. Your coach follows your lead.
Is this therapy?
No. We're not therapists and this isn't mental health support. We focus on team dynamics and management skills. If something comes up that's beyond our scope, we'll tell you.
