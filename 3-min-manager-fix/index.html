The CLOVER Framework™

The 6 Neural Triggers That Create Unstoppable Engagement
C COMMUNICATION
The Brain's Need for Clarity & Connection
❌ What's Failing: Information hoarding, one-way broadcasts, assumption-based decisions
✅ The 30-Second Fix: Start each day with "Here's what you need to know today..."
🧠 Why It Works: Reduces cortisol by 34%, builds psychological safety
L LEARNING
The Brain's Need for Growth
❌ What's Failing: Stagnant roles, no development path, same problems daily
✅ The 45-Second Fix: Weekly question: "What skill do you want to develop this week?"
🧠 Why It Works: Triggers neuroplasticity, increases retention by 87%
O OPPORTUNITY
The Brain's Need for Progress
❌ What's Failing: Dead-end positions, unclear advancement, external hires over promotion
✅ The 60-Second Fix: Monthly check: "What opportunity excites you most right now?"
🧠 Why It Works: Activates dopamine pathways, creates future focus
V VULNERABILITY
The Brain's Need for Authentic Connection
❌ What's Failing: "Professional" facades, fear of mistakes, trust deficits
✅ The 30-Second Fix: Share one challenge you're facing: "Here's where I need help..."
🧠 Why It Works: Builds oxytocin, creates psychological safety
E ENABLEMENT
The Brain's Need for Autonomy & Support
❌ What's Failing: Roadblocks, missing resources, responsibility without authority
✅ The 45-Second Fix: Daily question: "What's blocking you that I can remove?"
🧠 Why It Works: Reduces learned helplessness, increases ownership
R REFLECTION
The Brain's Need for Meaning & Recognition
❌ What's Failing: No celebration of wins, lessons lost, hamster wheel feeling
✅ The 30-Second Fix: Friday ritual: "This week's win was... because you..."
🧠 Why It Works: Consolidates learning, floods brain with dopamine
