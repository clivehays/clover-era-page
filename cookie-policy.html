🤔 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and our service more useful to you.

ℹ️ Key Points About Cookies
  • Cookies don't contain personal information like your name or email
  • They can't harm your device or install malware
  • Most cookies expire after your session or within a set timeframe
  • You have full control over which cookies you accept

Other Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other similar technologies including:

  • Web Beacons: Small graphics with unique identifiers to track user interactions
  • Local Storage: Technology that stores data locally on your device
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that's cleared when you close your browser

⚙️ How We Use Cookies

🇪🇺 GDPR Compliant Cookie Usage

Clover ERA uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To recognize you when you sign in and throughout your visit
  • Security: To support security features and detect malicious activity
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: To understand how you use our platform and improve our services
  • Performance: To monitor site performance and optimize loading times
  • Marketing: To deliver relevant content and measure campaign effectiveness
🔒 Your Privacy Is Protected

We follow the principle of data minimization and only use cookies that are necessary or that you've consented to. Essential cookies are required for basic functionality, while optional cookies require your explicit consent.

📊 Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize cookies based on their function and purpose. You can manage your preferences for each category except essential cookies, which are necessary for the website to function properly.

🔐
Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They're usually set in response to your actions, such as setting privacy preferences, logging in, or filling forms.

Examples:
  • Session cookies for user authentication
  • Security tokens to prevent CSRF attacks
  • Load balancing cookies for server optimization
  • Cookie consent preferences
📈
Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website and services.

Examples:
  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid)
  • Page view tracking
  • Time spent on pages
  • Navigation paths
⚡
Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences, language selection, and region. Without these, some features may not work properly.

Examples:
  • Language preferences
  • Dashboard layout settings
  • Accessibility preferences
  • Video player settings
🎯
Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show relevant ads on other sites. They work by uniquely identifying your browser and device.

Examples:
  • LinkedIn Insights
  • Facebook Pixel
  • Google Ads remarketing
  • Conversion tracking cookies

🎛️ How to Manage Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies on our website and in your browser:

1. Cookie Preference Center

Use our Cookie Preference Center to control which categories of cookies you accept. You can access this at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in our website footer.

2. Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

Chrome
Firefox
Safari
Edge

Google Chrome

  1. Click the three dots menu in the top right
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Privacy and security"
  4. Click "Cookies and other site data"
  5. Choose your preferred cookie settings

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Click the menu button and select "Settings"
  2. Select "Privacy & Security"
  3. Under "Cookies and Site Data", click "Manage Data"
  4. Select the cookies you want to remove
  5. Click "Remove Selected"

Safari

  1. Click "Safari" in the menu bar
  2. Select "Preferences"
  3. Click the "Privacy" tab
  4. Click "Manage Website Data"
  5. Select cookies and click "Remove"

Microsoft Edge

  1. Click the three dots menu in the top right
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Cookies and site permissions"
  4. Click "Manage and delete cookies and site data"
  5. Configure your preferences

3. Opt-Out Tools

You can also use these industry opt-out tools:

  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out
💡 Please Note

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not work properly without certain cookies enabled.

🚫 Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting that signals to websites that you don't want to be tracked. Currently, there is no universal standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals.

Clover ERA honors DNT signals by not loading non-essential tracking cookies when we detect this setting is enabled in your browser. However, essential cookies necessary for the website to function will still be used.
