How Daily Measurement Works

Simple process, powerful results

1

Morning Pulse Check

Employees receive a 30-second anonymous pulse check. One tap on mobile or desktop. No logins, no tracking, just honest feedback about how they're feeling today.

2

Real-Time Analysis

AI instantly analyzes responses, identifies patterns, and flags concerns. Managers see team sentiment trends without individual identification. Spot burnout risks before they escalate.

3

Immediate Action Alerts

When engagement dips or burnout signals appear, managers receive actionable recommendations. Address issues within 48 hours, not 6 months later when talent has already left.

4

Weekly Trend Reports

See how engagement fluctuates throughout the week. Identify patterns like Monday blues or Friday burnout. Understand your team's rhythms and optimize accordingly.

5

Monthly Deep Dives

Combine daily pulse data with monthly reflection sessions. Use quantitative trends to guide qualitative discussions. Create action plans based on real data, not assumptions.
