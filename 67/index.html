The CLOVER Framework: 6 Behavioral Dimensions That Predict Turnover

C Communication

Is information flowing both directions, or has someone stopped asking questions?

L Learning

Is someone still pushing for growth, or have they stopped asking for development?

O Opportunity

V Vulnerability

Does someone have what they need, or have they stopped escalating blockers?

Does someone feel seen, or have they gone from vocal to silent?
