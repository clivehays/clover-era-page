The Mechanism

What We Measure That You're Not Seeing

C
Communication

Is information flowing both directions, or has someone stopped asking questions?

L
Learning

Is someone still pushing for growth, or have they stopped asking for development?

O
Opportunity

Can someone see a future here, or have they stopped talking about next quarter?

V
Vulnerability

Can someone be honest without risk, or have they started "staying in their lane"?

E
Enablement

Does someone have what they need, or have they stopped escalating blockers?

R
Reflection

Does someone feel seen, or have they gone from vocal to silent?

When someone goes from "why are we doing it this way?" to "sounds good" to "just doing my job," you've got 30–60 days before they resign. We measure that shift daily.
