5 Ways to Beat Change Fatigue

Build resilience while maintaining momentum

Strategy #1
Change Triage
Stop everything. Prioritize only critical changes. Cancel or delay the rest.
Strategy #2
Recovery Periods
Build in stability zones between changes. Let teams catch their breath.
Strategy #3
Co-Creation
Involve teams in designing changes. Ownership reduces resistance.
Strategy #4
Small Wins
Break big changes into micro-victories. Celebrate progress frequently.
Strategy #5
Energy Management
Monitor team energy levels. Adjust pace based on capacity, not deadlines.
