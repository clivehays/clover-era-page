5 Ways to Boost Productivity Today

Quick wins for immediate impact

Booster #1
Time Blocking
Schedule focused work blocks. No meetings, no interruptions, just deep work.
Booster #2
Energy Mapping
Match high-value tasks to peak energy times. Save routine work for low-energy periods.
Booster #3
Two-Minute Rule
If it takes less than 2 minutes, do it now. Stop task accumulation.
Booster #4
Meeting Audit
Cut 50% of meetings. Make remaining ones 25% shorter with clear agendas.
Booster #5
Single-Tasking
Stop multitasking. Focus on one thing at a time for 40% better results.
