Important Reminders

These services are completely free, confidential, and staffed by trained professionals who understand mental health crises. You are not alone, and reaching out for help is a sign of strength.

If you're supporting someone else, stay with them when possible, listen without judgment, and help them connect with these resources. Remove any means of self-harm if it's safe to do so.

Your life matters. Help is available. Recovery is possible.
